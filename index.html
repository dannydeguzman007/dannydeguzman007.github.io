Stay in the Loop

Family Notifications

Three ways to make sure the whole family gets a nudge on their phone whenever something important happens — no new apps needed.

📧

Family Group Email

One email address that reaches everyone at once. Send it and all five of you get it in your Gmail inbox — which pops up on iPhone just like a text.

1
Go to groups.google.com → click "Create group" → name it Our Family and pick an address like ourfamily@googlegroups.com.
2
Click People → Members → Add members → add Mama, Vincent, Daniel and Jacob's Gmail addresses. They each get an invite to tap Accept.
3
Done! Now email that address whenever you want to reach everyone at once.
Set Up Group →
📅

Calendar Reminders

Once the shared Family Calendar is active, Google automatically emails everyone when a new event is added or coming up. Nothing extra to set up — it's built in.

1
Set up the Family Calendar (see the Calendar section above).
2
Each person can go to Google Calendar → Settings → Notifications to choose how far in advance they want reminders — 1 day, 1 hour, etc.
3
Reminders land in Gmail and show as iPhone notifications automatically.
Calendar Settings →
📱

Add Site to Home Screen

Save this site to your iPhone home screen so it looks and opens like an app — one tap and you're on the Family Hub. Do this on every family phone.

1
Open the Family Hub link in Safari on your iPhone (must be Safari, not Chrome).
2
Tap the share button (box with arrow ↑) at the bottom of the screen.
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. The site icon now lives on your home screen!
Do this in Safari on iPhone

💡 Make sure Gmail notifications are on: On each iPhone go to Settings → Notifications → Gmail and make sure "Allow Notifications" is switched on. This is what makes family emails and calendar reminders pop up on the lock screen.

Getting This Online

How to Publish on GitHub

GitHub is a free service where you host this page so everyone in the family can visit it anytime from any phone. No experience needed — follow these steps:

1

Create a free GitHub account

Go to github.com → click "Sign up." Use your email, pick a username like ourfamilyhub, and confirm your email.

2

Create a repository (a project folder)

Click the green "New" button. Name it exactly: yourusername.github.io. Tick "Add a README file" and click "Create repository."

3

Upload this file

Click "Add file" → "Upload files." Drag your index.html file in. Write "First upload" in the message box and click "Commit changes."

4

Switch on GitHub Pages

Click Settings at the top → find "Pages" in the left sidebar → under "Branch" choose main → click Save. Wait 2 minutes.

5

Your site is live! 🎉

Visit https://yourusername.github.io — it's online and free forever. Share that link with the whole family and bookmark it on every phone.

6

To update the site later

Go to your GitHub repository → click index.html → click the pencil ✏️ icon → make your changes → click "Commit changes." The live site updates in under a minute.
